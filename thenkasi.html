   Tenkasi - Kasi of the South. Surrounded with different rivers, situated on the foothills of the Western Ghats, Tenkasi’s natural beauty and scenic locales make it a great holiday destination. This is the perfect place to let loose, relax and find peace. Tenkasi, located in the state of Tamil Nadu, is famous for its light monsoon showers, one of the most enjoyable experiences in this city. The waterfalls add to the lush green backdrops and the soothing tunes of the drizzles, luring many a tourist from the world over.

Kalakkad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve

Kalakkad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve (KMTR) located in the Southern Western Ghats in Tenkasi district district in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu, is the second-largest protected area in Tamil Nadu. It is part of the Agasthyamala Biosphere Reserve.
